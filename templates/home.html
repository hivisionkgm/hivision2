A security alarm is a system designed to detect intrusion, such as unauthorized entry, into a building or other areas such as a home or Bank or school or Shopping complexes etc.... Security alarms used in residential, commercial, industrial, and military properties protect against burglary (theft) or property damage, as well as personal protection against intruders

Automatic gates consist of two basic components: Gate: The gate is the physical object that is moved to block the gate opening.
